Contact

We’d love to hear from you! If you have a question, you can ask any of our officers at practice or contact us electronically.

Have general inquiry questions? Email us at fencingofficers@umich.edu. We can help in person, too. Stop by during any practice and we’ll take care of you.
Want to anonymously give feedback, ask a question, raise a concern, etc.? Please use our suggestions box.
